The Taj Mahal 'Crown of the Palace', is an ivory-white marble mausoleum on the southern bank of the river Yamuna in the Indian city of Agra.

Petra originally known to its inhabitants as Raqmu or Raqēmō, is a historic and archaeological city in southern Jordan.

Machu Picchu is a 15th-century Inca citadel located in the Eastern Cordillera of southern Peru on a 2,430-meter (7,970 ft) mountain ridge.

The Colosseum Italian: Colosseo is an oval amphitheatre in the centre of the city of Rome, Italy, just east of the Roman Forum.
